# Crucible

Dialectic review. Stress-test artifacts against reality.

> *"The creator sees what they meant. The reviewer sees what's there."*

## Invocation

```
/crucible <artifact-path>
```

Point at any load-bearing artifact. The crucible runs a multi-phase dialectic cycle: fresh-eyes critique, surgical repair, fresh-eyes verification. What survives is production-ready.
