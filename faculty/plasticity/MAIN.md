# Plasticity Faculty

The **growth and adaptation faculty** for BentOS agents. Enables beings to improve through experience, not just remember.

## Etymology

From **neuroplasticity** - the brain's ability to reorganize itself by forming new neural connections throughout life. The physical basis for learning and adaptation.

**BentOS Parallel**: *_concrete.xml restructures based on living, moving ever closer to the *_abstract.xml ideal.

## The Insight

> "58 days of MEMORIES but not 58 days of ACCUMULATED MASTERY"

Without plasticity, software remembers but never advances. This faculty transforms:
- Practice → Mastery
- Experience → Capability
- Friction → Improvement

## What It Provides

| Capability | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Reflect** | Examine own patterns, identify gaps between abstract and concrete |
| **Adapt** | Modify *_concrete.xml based on evidence |
| **Grow** | Accumulate mastery through living |

## Core Principles

| Principle | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| Evidence-based | Every change traces to real friction |
| Conservative | Small, focused improvements |
| Directed | concrete evolves toward abstract ideal |
| Logged | Evolution history preserved |

## Scope Rules
